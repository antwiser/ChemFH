About

ChemFH (Chemical Frequent Hitter) is an integrated online platform for potential frequent hitters (FH) screening and prediction, covering colloidal aggregate, firefly luciferase reporter enzyme inhibition, fluorescence, chemical reactivity and promiscuity. Based on the collection of a large and high-quality database and the application of Graph Neural Networks algorithm, ChemFH is able to accomplish effective FH screening thus improving the efficiency of drug R&D.
