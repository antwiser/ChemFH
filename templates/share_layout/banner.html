ChemFH

ChemFH (Chemical Frequent Hitter) is an integrated online platform for the identification of potential frequent hitters, including colloidal aggregate, firefly luciferase reporter enzyme inhibition, fluorescence, chemical reactivity and promiscuity. Based on the collection of a large and high-quality database and the application of Graph Neural Networks architectures, the prediction models implemented in ChemFH are able to achieve reliable detection capability of frequent hitters, thus improving the efficiency of drug R&D.
