About

The website provides a precise and efficient service for identifying frequent hitter compounds. This functionality is based on a high-quality database comprising 810,000 entries across seven distinct frequent hitter mechanisms and employs a multi-task DMPNN model framework.

We utilize CSRF tokens (Cookies) to safeguard against cross-site request forgery attacks, a vital security measure for our website. We assure you that we respect your privacy and do not store or share any other personal information on your device.
